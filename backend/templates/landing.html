Features

Everything your team needs

A complete toolkit to plan, execute, and ship — without juggling a dozen separate apps.

Project Management

Organise all your work into projects. Track progress with visual indicators and keep every stakeholder in the loop.

Task Tracking

Create tasks, set priorities, assign due dates, and move them through your workflow as progress happens.

Team Collaboration

Assign tasks to teammates, manage roles, and see who owns what across every project in your workspace.

Kanban Board

Switch between list and board view. Drag tasks between Pending, In Progress, Review, and Completed in one click.

Live Dashboard

Get a birds-eye view of all activity — task completion rates, team workload, and project health at a glance.

Role-Based Access

Admins manage the workspace; members focus on their tasks. Secure, JWT-authenticated access throughout.
